Auditions

Students wishing to audition for the Interlude Orchestra should follow the steps below.

  1. Fill out the google survey HERE by March 22nd.
  2. Wait for audition materials to arrive via email Between March 23rd and 25th. Some instruments require solo playing for the auditions; students may look at these requirements and begin to work on this part before the excerpts are sent via email.
  3. See below for specific requirements regarding your instruments
  4. Make a VIDEO AUDITION and upload it to the google drive folder shared with you over email. Title your video (s) LAST NAME, INSTRUMENT, Interlude 2019. The video recording may be completed with a smartphone or other filming device with decent audio capabilities. We do not care about visual quality, although we would like to be able to see you when you are playing. You may make a video for each exerpt/solo if you wish. You do not need to make one long video.
  5. Upload your video audition by Monday, May 6th.
  6. Wait for an email that will list accepted members by May 13th. You will also receive an email with details about rehearsal locations and preparations for our first rehearsal.
  7. Please email Jack Reynertson at interludeorchestra@gmail.com with any questions you may have.
  8. We look forward to making great music with you!

Instrument Requirements:

Violin, Viola, Cello:

Excerpts. Principal: 90 seconds of playing on a solo work of the player’s choice, as well as additional excerpts

Bass:

Excerpts, 90 seconds of playing on a solo work of the player’s choice. Principal player auditionees will demonstrate 90 seconds of playing on a second solo of their choice.

Oboe:

Excerpts, 90 seconds of playing on a solo work of the player’s choice. *The Oboe player may perform some specific excerpts on English Horn if they play one. The Audition email will include transposed excerpts for both English Horn and Oboe; the student may elect to use either version.

Flute:

Excerpts, 90 seconds of playing on a solo work of the player’s choice. *The Flute player may perform specific Excerpts on piccolo if they play one.

Clarinet:

Excerpts, 90 seconds of playing on a solo work of the player’s choice. The Principal player auditionees will perform an additional 90 seconds of solo playing on a work of the player’s choice.

Bassoons:

Excerpts, 90 seconds of playing on a solo work of the player’s choice. The Principal player auditionees will perform an additional 90 seconds of solo playing on a work of the player’s choice.

Horn:

Excerpts, 90 seconds of playing on a solo work of the player’s choice. The Principal player auditionees will perform an additional 90 seconds of solo playing on a work of the player’s choice.
